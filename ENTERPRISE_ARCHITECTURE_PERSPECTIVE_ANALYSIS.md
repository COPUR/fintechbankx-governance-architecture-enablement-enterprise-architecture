# FinTechBankX Enterprise Architecture Perspective Analysis

## Business Architecture
- Capability map aligned to operating model and squad ownership.
- As-is/to-be organizational views capture governance and execution model changes.

## Application Architecture
- DDD bounded contexts and hexagonal architecture patterns are documented.
- Service decomposition and repository split support low coupling and high cohesion.

## Data Architecture
- Data-at-rest and credential/secrets governance are codified.
- Ownership and database naming patterns are captured in service catalog and manifest.

## Technology Architecture
- Kubernetes/service mesh and active-active deployment patterns are specified.
- CI/CD gate expectations are part of transformation and runbook artifacts.

## Security Architecture
- Keycloak-based AAA and FAPI/DPoP strategy are documented.
- mTLS, policy controls, and compliance evidence pathways are captured in design pack.

## Governance and Delivery
- Technical debt register and remediation plans are traceable.
- Migration runbook and cutover readiness scorecard provide operational decision gates.
